Forecasting the Tumbling 2020's news

In the year and half following October 2008 the stock market fell almost 60%. The housing market fell 75% in the two years following March 2007. Assets prices fell and investors rushed to invest in secure assets, mainly the government bonds. In June 2007 the interest rates started a sharp fall of more than 60% from 5.25% to 2% in one year and half. The low interest rates alerted investors of a possible inflation coming. Oil and gold more than doubled in the year and half following December 2008, when the US Federal central bank announced the first quantitative easing. A quantitative easing is when the central bank injects money directly into the economy by buying assets, mainly financial assets, bypassing the need to find entrepreneurs who take credit to insert new money into the circulating money. Bankers have injected like this $3,500 billion which is approximately 25% of the $14,000 billion existing.

“Finally fresh air” thinks Tumblien stretching after going out of the spaceship. He turns around, looks aside and listens to the birds singing. Then he decides to go collect some wood. Days forward Tumblien has been building a shelter close to the fertile land on which he landed. Soon he starts to plant vegetables and live from cultivating the ground and hunting other animals. This is the most basic way to satisfy needs. Any person in any time would able to do the same, either self-employed or employed by somebody who was already settled down. Tumblien choses to do it freely on his account.

Rapidly Tumblien is producing enough to satisfy more than he need. “Even after saving enough food for 3 weeks, there is still left much. I do not want the remaining to rot. As recommended by a passing peasant, from now on once a week he journeys to one of the closest villages and trades his left over in exchange for something else.

1. THE MARKET AND THE MAGIC MARKET

Noticing that products available in great quantity cost little and rare products cost more, he wonders why. “Some products are almost readily available in nature and others cost more time and effort to get them made” answers Jack the trader. He is told too that some magic products reduce the time needed to produce other products. “These magic products are similar to superpowers, they extend human abilities allowing one to do in one day what before took him seven” adds Jack that now looks more like a wizard than a trader. “Is there a magic product that could help me work the ground?” asks Tumblien full of curiosity. The magic trader disappears in the back store of the market stall. He comes back with an irrigation hose in his hands. Reminding all the hours he has spent going back and forth to the lake, Tumblien happily agrees to trade the hose for all his surplus. He thanks Jack and thinks to himself “Ah, what a great deal”. He becomes now an entrepreneur that owns products made to produce faster.

As the weeks pass Tumblien plants more land with his new free time from the irrigation hose. He increases his production to such an extent that he needs to go everyday to the market. Luckily he gets to trade his products for another magic product: a carriage that allows him to transport at once what he was used to take in 13 trips to the village. “With this new carriage I won’t need to go to the village everyday.” With time he starts liking the town and trading. He admires the talents of people at creating magic. He is very grateful for the existence of the market. “If I would invent some magic product, I would probably take it to the market” Tumblien thinks to himself “that is the crosspoint of most trades”. Consequently he decides to rent a market store with the intent to be among the firsts to discover new magic products. Also he hosts 2 persons that agree to work with him. “I will teach you all you have to do, you can use my shelter, I will move to the village and provide you with all you need trading our production” tells the two workers that noded.

Every morning he brings the product of the piece of land where he lived. During the day he trades them for other products. And the day after he brings back products to the persons who work with him, as well as magic products for the farm. His farm keeps growing and in as little as 1 year 14 persons are working with him. Tumblien feels very lucky. He organizes the farm and the store. When others don’t know what he wants them to do they check with him.

In his store and on the land a huge amount of accumulated products that were traded in for what was brought to the market are piling up. “We have 5 complete tablewares, meters of tissue to make clothes, clothes, some barrels of delicious drink, wardrobes, decorated furnitures...” counts Tumblien. These products are wealth. Valuable things, that would cost time and work to be produced, if he didn’t already have them. He’s got to be a wealthy businessmen.

2. ENTER THE SCENE MONEY

A random night Tumblien meets his wife. Years forward they get married and bring some children. As his littles grow up, he is concerned: “As better magic products are produced, people is able to make all products at a lower cost of time and effort… consequently all products are constantly becoming available in greater quantity. If the products I own are becoming available in greater quantity their price is falling...”. So he concludes his wealth is loosing value. “I don’t want my wealth to loose all its value. I would like to let something for the kids”. Accordingly he desires to trade his wealth for things he believe will not loose value through time. “How other wealthy business men store their wealth?” asks around Tumblien in the market place. “The most wealthy men build big castles. Also they order pieces of art like portraits, gold jewelry and silver tableware” Jack tells him . “Gold and silver have the best properties to store wealth” judges Tumblien, “they last long in time without loosing any property, they can be divided in small pieces to match any value in trade and can be transported without risk of damage, not like castles or paintings”. Investing his wealth in order to avoid loosing value, Tumblien has become an investor.

Before long he is only accepting silver and gold in exchange for his products. He uses the metals as a medium to store the value of wealth while better magic products and products in greater quantity appear in the market. “I will call products that keep value… money products”. The practice of trading against only one good has some advantages: everybody knows what to bring to Tumblien’s store in order to take food: metals. Time passing, the fashion spreads and now all merchants store their wealth in money metals. The vocabulary is accordingly expanded: “trade” is replaced by two verbs: “buy” and “sell”. Buy means bringing money metals to the market expecting to take other products. Sell means bringing products to the market expecting to receive money products for them. Everybody in the horizon is now using metals as money products. Because of storing value a new use is found for metals: they are the wild card product convertible against any other with great acceptance. Also it that can be stored up for future use.

3. INDUSTRIAL REVOLUTION TRIGGER

Tumblien expresses his thought as such “money is the most liquid product, it can be turned into anything that has been produced by humans. Money is the way to stay wealthy. It stores the value of products while better magic products appear and products are available in greater quantity. As more people uses metals as money, the same quantity will be split among more people. So metals become rarer and more valuable the more people use them as money. Furthermore as products become available in greater quantity they will cost less. Therefore the more abundant are products, the more valuable becomes money”. Tumblien believes money will become very valuable product forward in time so security will be a concern.

“How other wealthy men safe guard their money?” wonders Tumblien. “Since money is such a valuable product, I could start up a safe deposit of money. I will call it bank.” The new bank is not very welcome in the market. However Tumblien gets a brilliant idea to make it run. “The fashion would spread if the wealthiest merchants use the Bank receipts as money instead of metals” believes Tumblien. Indeed as wealthy merchants are seen by most of the public using bank receipts to pay their employees, people starts depositing their money at the bank and using receipts or account checkbooks too. They call these receipts bills or bank bills. People start indifferently confusing them with metals money, and calling them bill money. Tumblien gets to be known in the marketplace as the banker.

Tumblien realizes people rarely come to search their real money, the metals. Instead they get used to the lightness of bills. The real money stays in the safe-deposit box. Years forward the level of real money in the safe didn’t fell once under 80% of the peek. And actually only rose year over year. He gets another brilliant idea “I will lend this idle money to entrepreneurs and wealthy business men to buy magic products like I did decades ago”. He lends not the actual metal money but rather issues new bills. The only things he gets in exchange for the new bills is a written commitment from the entrepreneur to pay back the money and some interests. Around him wealthy business men blossom: having received a loan and purchased state-of-the-art magic goods, these business men get to reduce their cost of time and work under that of any other competitor. Tumblien reflects on the importance of the magic products, “All these business men have been able to succeed like me thanks to the magic products. Being able to use the latest magic products they lowered the cost of time and effort to produce. They have increased the quantity of products available, and consequently lowered the price of these products below those of any previous competitor. Magic products are really very important, I will call them capital products, as they lead the quantity and price of products”.

Around Tumbliens’ people the quantity of products increases and people gets to live longer. People moves from the country to the city in order to share the products. The quantity of people doubles every few decades. The value of metals money only increases. Furthermore the success of Tumbliens network yields its profits: the triumph of entrepreneurs brings additional metal money every year. The banker sees convenient to expand and to make other banks wherever there is people willing to trade their magic products. “The network has been growing fast. We are the biggest bank in the world. We are related to the most up to date businesses that lead the production providing others with products at the lowest price. Thanks to the market and to all people that present us their capital products, we are able to provide them with the low cost products that all people likes and needs. The system is burgeoning” Says Tumblien to Jack in a enthusiastic though skeptic voice tone.

4. FINANCE TAKEOVER

Banks multiply. Not all bankers are prudent and some issue too many bills. When a bank issues more bills than it has real money, it risks that there will not be enough real money when depositors come asking to withdraw their deposit. Tumblien notices each time a bank goes bankrupt it becomes a problem for all the others. People looses trust in all banks when they see one failing. To remove weak points Tumblien come with another great idea: “It is in the interest of all banks to join forces. All banks depends on the reputation of others. We can create a bank of banks. Call it central bank. All the real money of banks will be stored there. Other banks will then store mainly bills of the central bank. And people will use either central bank bills or other bank accounts checkbooks”. Tumblien assesses that the only real threat to such banking system is if all clients claim to convert their central bank bills into real money at once, the central bank wouldn’t have enough real money. Nevertheless he goes ahead with the project and succeeds to establish the central Bank among banks.

At this stage Tumblien upgrades to mind shaper: he is wealthy, mighty among the most successful business men and wants to lock in his position. How does he do that? Tumblien walking think in his head “Most credit, new bill-money issued without real-money deposited, is granted to buy capital products. Consequently when the bank system grants credits to entrepreneurs the prices of capital products will increase first. The prices of all goods follow some time after, as the new money filters to the employees of capital product companies. Because employees spend in a great variety of products, all product prices increase then. If the bank system earns 5% yearly on loans, and prices increase 10% in a year, the bill money lent will have lost product value when returned to the bank.” Furthermore he remarks that the larger the value fall of the bill money (the increase in prices of products) the more people that notices there’s something wrong with the bill. But also as better magic products are used to make products available in greater quantity, product prices will fall progressively. Noticing the rise of prices arrives earlier than the fall Tumblien thinks “I need to prevent people from noticing the value of the bill is devaluating. Or people will want to convert their bills back into real money” Tumblien sends an order to banks to rise the interest on loans so that entrpreneurs reduce their loan taking. The effect of such a measure is that capital products enterprises see their revenue tumble as no business men comes to buy, since they stop taking loans. Eventually, these capital products companies lower the salary of employees and strive to continue operations at a lower cost.

Tumblien’s idea has worked: as business men give back their credit, bills pile up in bank. Prices fall double: once because of the greater availability of products and second because of the drain of money from the circulation to the banks. He’s happy to see his action has hit two targets with one shot “Prices of capital products are now falling sharply providing us the perfect opportunity to acquire capital goods at a low price. Besides the devaluation of the bill money has reverted itself to a revaluation and my advantage in the money game will remain unnoticed”. Tumblien is now in place to keep businesses around him at the state-of-the-art production methods at a low investment price. And he does very well in this job. Around him business becomes even more profitable when they are able to buy capital product at a cheap price. However he is preoccupied that no entrepreneur wants to take a loan in times in which product prices fall. Entrepreneurs fear there prices will continue falling and revenues will not root up again.

TUMBLIEN’S TABLE TO SUM UP ECONOMIC PHASES THAT CAUSED BY MONETARY/FINANCIAL CHANGES:

Phase Description Implications
Inflation period in which bill money increases by issuing new bills as credit granting Inflation allows him and the business men around him to acquire the best capital products in the market – but rising prices make people distrust the bill money and ask for the deposited real money.
Deflation period in which bill money is drained from the circulation to the banks by raising interest rates. He detects that both imply advantages and risks Deflation allows to acquire the capital products at the lowest price but makes revenues, and so wages and profits, to fall. Falling prices makes business men delay starting up or expanding businesses

Tumblien runs the most sophisticated way to satisfy his needs. He has created a digital accounting system that has replaced trade for real money. Through the power of money he’s able to bid up for market products and generate scarcity or prevent others to bid up and generate excess. In other words his influence in the account system allows him to inflate or deflate the available quantity of money. “I can arm business men with the best capital products. I can influence prices through inflation and deflation. Besides, the deflation is always greater than the previous inflation as it contains additional interests that were not introduced during inflation. So in order to maintain the quantity of circulating money stable the inflation must be bigger for each subsequent deflation”.

Of course, his network make him finance academics and mass media that plead his system. They gain popularity in any new country by financing prestigious people, like the kings and emperors. In the face of trouble to recover the debts, they also finance their ideological enemies to discredit the current authority and make a revolution. Sometimes split ideas evolve in a civil war. Tumblien is able to decide which side wins by financing them. Financing them means providing them with the most massive production methods known by his network that extends beyond the horizons. They promote the concept of nations like territories delimited by borders. They back the ideas of democratic governments for freedom, with human rights, a noble ideal nobody can deny. But the governments end up being the way to lend securely, he says “Lending to a government is perfect, it is as good as lending to all people compulsorily and make them pay back through taxes forward”. They lobby policies that will lock the system as raising taxes and making them payable only with bill money, confiscate real money and fine those who use it, and stop the convertibility of the bill money into real money. They are really influencer as by financing one party or another they decide the most known party and the most likely to be elected.

Also they promote a convoluted regulation that no one reads in life. This regulation will allow them to pay the minimum taxes and earn the most profits. Basically Tumblien employs think tanks to think how to optimize and protect the system he had created.

5.OVER-EXPANSION AND BACK TO NATURE.

The system Tumblien created is rooting up. His network is constantly monitoring new innovations and are the firsts to invest in new techniques. As the technique spread to other companies the the profits dilute. Consequently entrepreneurs are continuously renovating themselves with the latest capital products. System agents are sent to other lands looking for new creative magic. These agents are also used to inform Tumblien on the possibility of Banking there, the kind of government there is, if any, and the prestigious institutions that lead people’s beliefs. “To keep the system growing we need to keep increasing the quantity of people that uses the central Bank bills and the quantity of products in the market place available to trade against bank bills.”

Decades forward Tumblien and his network spreads to many other territories and peoples. Their degree of control is different in every place. In order to be liked by the different peoples they create different names for the bills in each nation. But the network is the same and they’re all connected and collaborating towards the same purpose: control the magic. Even though they grow rapidly they do not reach to completely install their system in every nation. In many nations the local power is hostile to Tumblien network. In others they accept it for production purposes but set up a similar banking system. And some surrender completely and collaborate with Tumblien system provided they can have loans. Loans that sometimes get stolen by the men in government before the taxed people receives any benefit. The Earth gets filled of people speaking about money and the benefits of capital products. Though not many get to understand them the way Tumblien does, peoples race to acquire or develop the latest capital products. Often wars happen between local peoples that do not want to surrender to Tumblien’s network and the now global network. Initially Tumblien didn’t want to make wars. He just wanted to secure a living for his children. But people in his network are trained to implement the safest strategy to the greatest profits that implies supervising others’ growth. Thanks to their production techniques and followers, Tumblien network is well prepared to win the wars and wins. The defeated nations agree to follow Tumblien network’s rules “for the peace of the world”.

Suddenly, a key reversal for Tumblien’s network is the birth of computers and the Internet. Internet has brought new ways to communicate that challenge Tumblien network in 2 main ways. On the one hand the Internet allows anyone to create content and publish it to the world. One of the strengths of Tumblien network is being able to control the advertising medias and the influencing scholars that make public education subejct. But Tumblien’s network needs to be able to address directly the influencers of a people to get their favors. Now with the arrival of Internet the content creation is distributed among too many influencers to orchestrate them all. Furthermore the prestige and popularity of influencers is much more ephemere and dynamic than in the old academic and medias institutions so it’s more difficult to choose influencers partners. The techniques and ideas travel instantly all around the world and the system of agents deployed to collect information is not anymore an advantage. On the other hand the computational power of computers allows to create public cryptography accounting systems, called blokchains. These systems are very similar to Tumblien’s system in that they create an accounting book of units. Blockchains differ from Tumblien system in that they do not need an intermediary (a Tumblien), the real money backing the virtual “bill” money units can be any product (not only gold and silver) and anybody is able to replicate the system as the code is open source. Basically anyone can now create his own central bank accounting book and digitals “bills”.

The actual story of Tumblien is goes viral through the Internet. As people becomes aware of how they were fooled they steadily want to start using metals and blockchain tokens as money away from governments and central banks money. But not all people is convinced and many still believe Tumblien’s network. Many profit from it. Many provide their children with food thanks to the system. These are afraid of giving up the comfort of the familiar day-to-day for anything else. Many have strong beliefs and little time that can barely reflect clearly without being demanded or distracted by their lives. However some people got to know the true Tumblien.

Tumblien misused popularity and prestige to embezzle real money. Doing wrong and felt the need to protect himself from innocent people. He disposed of the latest capital products without bringing anything to the market. For decades he mislead peoples into confusion bribing academics and politicians and buying communication medias that united to his network. He made leaders forgo their fundamental principles in order to speed their individual success helped by his network. He influenced institutions leaders in order to join the network of face the consequences. By wanting to impose his system he has generated discord among peoples who lived well before. Civil wars have sprung between his global network followers and local people who denied any need to install such a catchy system. The young people who discover his story, then travel and and look for a free land where to settle and live far from his dominating system. The ones engaged into a life project hardly can imagine how to adapt, so fear the change. The older seem indifferent and think they just won’t see the system fall.

In his room of ideas Tumblien is now in the dilemma of whether he really wants to keep growing his system. He is concerned that by growing his system he disturbs innocent livings. But there’s a whole network of millions of people on guard expecting him to keep illuminating the path to further power and control. To further order. At the top of the pyramid he oversees all operations, being informed to take decisions. He is constantly presented with the prospect of creating more terrorism and wars. For his network, peoples are more receptive when they are afraid, angry or weak so they promote wars. And that is the story of how Tumblien, the alien hacked the economy, that is, the way people satisfy their needs.
