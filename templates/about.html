The Tumbling 20's

This website aims to alert people about the Tumbling 2020's depression and to offer a safe heaven blockchain-based asset: the T20. The money brought to the T20 fund will be invested in financial markets and cryptocurrencies markets accounting for the decline of the financial system and the rise of blockchain companies during the 2020's.

Cyril Attie

Ten years ago, when I was 14 years old, the Great Recession stroke the economy in 2008. For 5 years I heard everywhere that crisis had happened and the economy was doing poorly. I watched in the news stock market traders pulling their hair away, people demonstrating in the streets of New York and Madrid and long queues at every Greek ATM machine. During my first year in college studying physics I decided to understand what really happened. So I droped and started my journey to understand what's a crisis and how to prevent it. I have conducted my investigation in private banking departments, trading and dealing rooms and brokerage firms. Focusing on monetary economics and the financial system, as they are the cause of crises, I stumbled upon Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies. I authored an investigation of the impact of bitcoin in the monetary system in December 2017 .
