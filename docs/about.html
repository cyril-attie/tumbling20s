What is a Crisis?

Ten years ago, when I was 14 years old, the Great Recession stroke the economy. For 5 years I heard everywhere that crisis had happened and the economy was doing poorly. I watched in the news people demonstrating in the streets of New York and Madrid, long queues at every Greek ATM and stock market traders pulling their hair away. During my first year in college studying physics I decided to give me a chance tp understand what really happened. So I droped and started my journey to understand what's a crisis. It's been now 6 years since I read my first economic book. I have passed through private banking departments, trading and dealing rooms and brokerage firms. I have thoroughly investigated monetary economics. Learnt to program to simulate investment stretegies and make arbitrage in cryptocurrencies. I wrote my bachelor thesis on the impact of bitcoin in the monetary system. Hopefully soon finding my way through the wealth management industry to have enough time hack and learn.
